Welcome to the SDA-Lab

Welcome to the official website of the Structural Dynamics and Assessment Laboratory (SDA-Lab) at University College Dublin. Our group focuses on pioneering research in structural dynamics, health monitoring, and data-driven assessment of civil infrastructure. We are dedicated to developing innovative solutions for ensuring the safety and resilience of structures such as bridges, buildings, and wind turbines. Explore our website to learn more about our research, publications, and the people behind our work.

- Dr. Abdollah Malekjafarian, Associate Professor

Research Focus

Structural Dynamics & Random Vibrations

Investigating the dynamic behavior of structures under operational and extreme loading conditions using analytical, numerical, and experimental methods.

Transport Infrastructure

Developing novel monitoring and assessment techniques for transport infrastructure, including bridges and railways, to enhance safety, and optimise maintenance.

Offshore Renewable Energy

Addressing the challenges in the design, monitoring, and assessment of offshore wind turbines and other renewable energy systems to ensure their long-term performance.
